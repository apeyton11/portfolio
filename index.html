About Me

Originally from California, I graduated from Wesleyan University with a BA in Psychology and a great deal of experience in Ethnomusicology and Biology. I spend most of my time thinking about how to make the human experience simpler, and I believe good design is integral to this goal, especially on the web.

After several years in the customer service industry, where I found joy in working with people to address concerns and solve problems, I decided I needed to delve deeper into the world of online human behavior, with the end goal of minimizing cognitive stressors and subconscious pain points. I strive to build simple, intuitive, and highly functional content.
